Our Story

My name is Isaac and I LOVE baking! But the real star of the show is my business partner, who bakes the best Mooncakes in the world. She is also my mum and we both have the Chinese character 林 (Lam) in our names. I couldn't be more proud of her for finally giving London an opportunity to taste some proper good Mooncakes! Don't just take my word for it, try our Mooncakes for yourself. After all, I'm only really here to help out with the computer stuff!
