Our Story

My name is Isaac and I LOVE baking! But the real star of the show is my business partner, who bakes the best Mooncakes in the world. She is also my mum and we both have the Chinese character 林 (Lam) in our names. I couldn't be more proud of her for finally giving Londoners an opportunity to taste some proper good Mooncakes! Don't just take my word for it, try our Mooncakes for yourself. After all, I'm only really here to help out with the computer stuff!

月餅 [Mooncakes]

Mooncakes are traditional Chinese treats enjoyed during 中秋節 (Mid-Autumn Festival). We like to keep things simple so have stuck to the classic flavours and we make almost everything from scratch! Our 蓮蓉 (lotus seed paste) is made from 100% lotus seeds and we do our toasting for the 五仁 (assorted nuts). And of course, we also soak our own salted egg for that perfect moon-like yolk!
